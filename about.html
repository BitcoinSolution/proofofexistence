What is proof of existence?

Use our free service to anonymously and securely store an online proof of existence for any document. Your documents are NOT stored online, so you don't have to worry about your data leaking. All we store is a cryptographic digest of the file, associated to the time in which you upload the document. In this way, you can later certify that said data existed at that time.

Common uses

Demonstrating data ownership without revealing actual data.
You can publicly reveal the digest and if conflict arises you can prove you had the data that generates the digest. Useful for copyrighted material, patents, etc.
Checking for document integrity.
If you store a proof for your document and later re-upload it, the system will only recognize it if it is completely and fully the same document. Changing the font of one letter (if it is a written doc), changing a single pixel (if it is an image), or any other slightest change, and we'll recognize it is different and generate other cryptographic digest.
Document timestamping.
You can prove certain data exists at a certain moment of time. This currently requires people to trust this site, but will soon use the Bitcoin blockchain to timestamp documents, as an optional feature for a small fee (required to push transactions into the Bitcoin network).
